## Key Operating Metrics (illustrative anchors)
- Scale: 17M+ connected vehicles, 450k+ vehicle adds per month (public press signals).
- Reach: 190+ countries with hundreds of MNO partners; focus on attach success, latency, throughput, and roaming performance.
- Growth levers: partner footprint expansion, higher data plans, upsell to infotainment/payments/analytics, and OTA reliability.

## Cost & Investment Themes (demo assumptions)
- Major cost lines: connectivity/roaming spend with MNO partners, platform/edge infrastructure, device/SIM provisioning, security/compliance.
- Efficiency levers: partner/QoS tuning, intelligent traffic steering, analytics-driven capacity planning, automation.
- Cash uses: platform R&D, partner expansion, data/privacy/security investments, and compliance-readiness (e.g., VoLTE/VoNR lawful intercept).

## Compliance & ESG (summary)
- Data privacy, security, and compliance for roaming/VoLTE/VoNR; lawful intercept readiness.
- Responsible operations: sustainable footprint considerations for global connectivity and data handling.
