# Cubic³ Partner Guide (Demo)

## Positioning
- SDV connectivity platform with global roaming reach (190+ countries, 550+ networks) and analytics/experience layers for OEMs and partners.
- Partner-friendly: service providers, integrators, and payments/content partners can bundle managed services using Cubic³ connectivity, telemetry, and UX tools.

## Core Offers
- **OEM Connectivity**: embedded connectivity with attach/latency SLAs and QoS telemetry.
- **Infotainment/StreamLocal³**: localised content delivery with QoS governance.
- **Driver Experience/DriverConnect³**: experience insights, churn reduction, proactive support signals.
- **Payments/FleetWallet³**: secure in-vehicle payments with compliance readiness.
- **Analytics/Explore³**: consumption insights, anomaly detection, OEM benchmarking.

## Target Segments
- Automotive OEMs and their program leads.
- Integrators and managed service providers supporting OEM rollouts.
- Payments/content partners aligning to in-vehicle experiences.
- MNO partners seeking premium/QoS connectivity programs.

## Partner Success Focus
- Activation speed: SIM/eSIM provisioning, clear SLAs, proactive partner capacity planning by country.
- Experience: attach/latency/jitter reporting; managed QoS options; rapid incident comms.
- Co-marketing: OEM program wins, infotainment launches, and payments/UX success stories.
- Integration: APIs/OSS for provisioning, telemetry, ticketing, and analytics feeds.

## Metrics Partners Care About (examples)
- Attach success and latency by country/partner/OEM program.
- Throughput and capacity headroom by region and partner.
- Activation/OTA lead times and incident response times.
- NPS/CSAT and churn risk signals by program.
